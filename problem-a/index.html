Prince Zuko

More information about Zuko

For this assignment, I chose to create a website for Prince Zuko, a character from Avatar: the Last Airbender, a Nickelodeon show that premiered in 2005. I decided to write Prince Zuko as he has a very interesting character arc and development throughout the three seasons of the show. Initially, Zuko starts out as a villain and the show’s main antagonist. He is on a mission to capture the Avatar, the show’s protagonist, who is destined to save the world from the fire nation.

Throughout the course of the show, the viewer is able to watch Zuko struggle, grow, and develop into a very likable character. He shifts from being the main villain searching to regain his “honor”, to a good guy who befriends the Avatar and aiding him in his mission of saving the world.

Avatar: the Last Airbender is one of my favorite shows of all time and I believe Prince Zuko greatly contributed to the success of the show.

Reasons to Admire Zuko

  1. Complex backstory
  2. Best character development
  3. Very motivated, although sometimes misplaced
  4. Is related to Iroh
